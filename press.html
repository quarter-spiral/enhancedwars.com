Description

Enhanced Wars is an asynchronous, turn-based strategy game for PC, Mac, Linux and web browser. Challenge your friends or make new ones in a tactical battle that unfolds at your pace. Created by a team of experienced professionals, Enhanced Wars delivers a familiar turn-based strategy game with fresh innovations to address some of the design foibles of the genre classics we are inspired by.

Our goal with Enhanced Wars is to build a fast, fun and accessible turn-based strategy game. A game that focuses on battle and tactics over troop build up and resource hoarding. A game that pays homage to the past while delivering a fresh, new experience.

At a basic level, Enhanced Wars will be familiar to any fan of turn-based strategy games. On your turn you deploy troops, position your army, capture strategic locations and attack the enemy. Players trade turns until one is victorious. But by integrating subtle changes like the Action Points system and the victory condition, Enhanced Wars delivers a unique take on turn-based strategy games.

History

Enhanced Wars is the first game from independent studio Quarter Spiral. It has been featured in the CODAME Indie Game developer showcase. The Kickstarter campaign that will determine the fate of Enhanced Wars was launched in August of 2013.

About Quarter Spiral

Quarter Spiral is an independent game developer founded in 2012 by game & web development veterans Ethan Levy, Alex Kohlhofer and Thorben Schroder. The team joined together to build technology that would enable independent game developers to transform games into businesses. After 9 months of building technology, building awareness and generally playing the silicon valley game, it was clear that the team was going to go broke begging for money.

In April of 2013 they started making Enhanced Wars, a multiplayer, turn-based strategy game built on the foundation they had developed over the past year. The cross-functional, 3 man team has contributed to over 35 shipped games and claims to have 2 game designers, 2 producers, 3 entrepreneurs, 1.5 graphic designers, 2.5 web developers, 1.5 game programmers, 1 server side expert, 1 business guy, 1 cook and 2 people capable of dunking a basketball if they so desired. The team is spread out between San Francisco, Raleigh/Durham and Hamburg, Germany and communicates daily via the magic of teh interwebz.
