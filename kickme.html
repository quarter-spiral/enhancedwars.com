What is Kickstarter?

Kickstarter is a crowd-funding platform where creators like me ask people to contribute money to their creative project. The creator makes a page pitching their project to the community, sets a fundraising goal and a timeline. If people like the project, they can pledge money to make it happen. If the project succeeds in reaching its funding goal, all backers' credit cards are charged when time expires. If the project falls short, no one is charged.

Kickstarter is an all-or nothing proposition. If our campaign ends and we have not reached our goal, no credit cards are charged and we get nothing. In order to reach it’s goal, Enhanced Wars will need many contributions, mostly from strangers who are interested in the game.

Why should I back Enhanced Wars?

Successful Kickstarter projects build their initial momentum with backing from friends and family. Even a contribution of $1 makes a huge difference. When a stranger sees that a project has a lot of backers, it gives him more confidence that Enhanced Wars is worth backing. So don’t delay, please back Enhanced Wars immediately.

Does it really work?

Absolutely! As of August, 2013 over 2,000 games projects have succeeded on Kickstarter, raising over $144 million. One well known project is Torment which raised over $4 million from over 74,000 backers. On a smaller scale, friends of Quarter Spiral have raised over $20,000 each with games Dashkin and Corporate America.

Can you raise more than your goal?

Yes, many successful projects on Kickstarter raise more than their fundraising goal. If we are lucky enough to raise more than $50,000 the money will go towards building a better game. More money will translate into being able to spend more time on Enhanced Wars before it is launched adding features and polishing the game.

What's in it for me?

You mean our ETERNAL GRATITUDE isn’t enough? Well in that case you can choose one of the many reward tiers we have set up for Enhanced Wars. When you look at the Kickstarter campaign, you will see a number of rewards based upon the amount of money you pledge to the project. Rewards include copies of the game, early access to Enhanced Wars and other digital goodies targeted at fans of the game.

Kickstarter is a patronage platform, not an investment platform. You should back Enhanced Wars because you want to help us succeed, not because of an expectation of monetary reward.

If you are not interested in any of the rewards and just want to see us succeed, you can pledge any amount you want without choosing a reward.

What happens if the campaign fails?

Quarter Spiral has been self funded for the past year and we are running out of money. If the Kickstarter campaign is unsuccessful we will go out of business and be unable to complete Enhanced Wars unless an alternate funding source magically appears. We are not planning for magic, so please back us today!

About Quarter Spiral

In its 40+ year history, the games industry has been in constant flux. In recent years, almost every aspect of making games has changed alongside an explosion of platforms, practices and players. But publishing remains largely unfazed.

Quarter Spiral was founded on a simple idea. Publishing should be a service that puts developers and players before profit. Publishing should serve independence and innovation. Publishing should put you in control of your destiny.
